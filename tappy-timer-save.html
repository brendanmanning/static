Tappy Timer 12+

Productivity is a tap away!

Kelly Manning

    • Free

iPhone Screenshots

Description

Keep track of what matters most! Reading that new book? Taking a coffee break? Knowing how long you spend on each makes managing your time easier!


How to: When you start a new activity, tap its icon in Tappy Timer. When you're finished, tap the icon again to stop. We'll remember how long you spent doing it even if you close the app!


Features:


> Unlimited Activities

Save as many activities as you want!


> Groups

Organize activities into groups


> Charts

See the breakdown for how you spend your time every day, week, and month. Tap the chart to toggle between name, percentage of time, and hours spent.


> Customize

Personalize everything with beautiful colors and a wide selection of modern icons
